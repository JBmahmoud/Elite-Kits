Our Journey

2021 · The Beginning
Elite Kits is Founded
Starting from a small garage in Tripoli, Lebanon, we launched with just 30 jersey designs across 2 leagues. Our mission: make great football kits affordable for Lebanese fans.
2022 · Going Bigger
Expanded to 4 Leagues
We added La Liga, Serie A, and Bundesliga to our Premier League offering. Our first 500 orders shipped. Customer satisfaction rating: 4.8 out of 5.
2023 · New Features
Custom Printing Launched
We introduced our custom name and number printing service for just +$5. Fans can now truly make a jersey their own. We also launched 14-day hassle-free returns.
2024 · Going Digital
Elite Kits Website Launches
Our full e-commerce platform launched featuring 500+ jerseys, the world leagues collection, admin dashboard, and real-time stock management.
2025 · Today
2025/26 Season Collection
We now carry the full 2025/26 season collection 6 top leagues, 500+ jersey options, a wishlist system, live chat, smart size guide, and much more. Just getting started.
