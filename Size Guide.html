How to Measure

  • 1
    Chest / Bust

    Wrap the tape around the fullest part of your chest, keeping it horizontal and snug but not tight.

  • 2
    Shoulder Width

    Measure from the tip of one shoulder across the back to the tip of the other shoulder.

  • 3
    Jersey Length

    Measure from the highest point of the shoulder down to your preferred length (usually hip-level).

  • 4
    Pro Tip

    If you're between two sizes, choose the larger one for a more comfortable, authentic matchday fit.

Fit Tips

In-Between Sizes?

Size up for a relaxed feel or choose the smaller size for a snug, athletic fit.

Match Fit vs Fan Fit

Player match jerseys run slim. Fan/replica editions offer a more relaxed silhouette.

Free Exchanges

If your size isn't right, we offer hassle-free exchanges within 14 days of delivery.

Custom Printing
